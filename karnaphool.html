Karnaphool

Background

A newly established brand, that has shopping experience only on Instagram profile. The business is growing fast and the demand from customers is also increasing.

Problem

Customers can only purchase from Instagram. Users have to contact the seller on Instagram by direct message and then the order is placed. No way of tracking the product ordered and have to contact the seller every time for updates.

Solution

Having an online shopping experience on a website will help users browse every product effectively and will also be able to track the order and won’t have to contact the seller every time. Users can also add products to their wishlist.

Low & Mid Fidelity Prototypes

High Fidelity Prototype

The prototype provided screens for home page, the products list view and a detailed view of the product. The users can easily browse through the website with the navigation on top and having the filter option in the product list page. Having a closer look at the product’s image, it will help users view the product details and an easy CTA button allowing users to add the product in the cart.
