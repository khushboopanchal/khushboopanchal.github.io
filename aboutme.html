A little about me…

I'm currently a graduate student of HCI at SUNY Oswego. I love creating a better user experience for the products I design while studying human behaviour.

I've a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and have an experience of working as a Web Developer for more than three years.

As a designer and a developer, I understand that a user-interface should be accessible to the end-user as well as look pretty. I am interested in creating designs which not only solves the problem of viewing it on every devices but also accessible to everyone using those devices.

College Loops Helping women on campus on their journey in computing

With my journey in Computer Science, I have seen that this field is male dominated. To bridge this gap, I started an organization on campus which supported women passionate about computing, technology, solving problems, and social change.
