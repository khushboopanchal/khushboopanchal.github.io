Hello! my name is Khushboo!

I'm a UX Designer and a Front-End Developer, currently a graduate student of Human-Computer Interaction at State University of New York, Oswego.

Why choose me?

As a designer and a developer, I understand that a user-interface should look pretty as well as it should allow the user to achieve its goal.

I believe, in order to have a successful product, studying human behaviour is a critical process.

What I do?

A curious designer, focused on creating designs for better user-experience. I like to create user-centric, pleasent and eye-catching designs.
