Khushboo Panchal

Hello! I'm UI/UX Designer, based in sunny San Jose, CA.

A little about me…

I'm currently a graduate student of HCI at SUNY Oswego. I love creating a better user experience for the products I design studying human behavior.

I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and have experience of working as a Web Developer for more than three years.

As a designer and a developer, I understand that a user-interface should be accessible to the end-user as well as look artistic. I am interested in creating designs which not only solves the problem of viewing it on every device but also accessible to everyone using those devices.

With my journey to Computer Science, I have seen that this field is male-dominated. To bridge this gap, I started an organization - College Loops on campus which supported women passionate about computing, technology, problem solving, and social change.

Read about my contribution to College Loops event series on LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook.
